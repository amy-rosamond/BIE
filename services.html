Inspection and Testing

E.I.C.R

Electrical Installation Condition Report

  1. It is recommended that every 5 years that all electrical installations are tested to ensure they remain safe. This includes giving a detailed report of any potential dangers in your wiring.
  2. It is also required when letting, buying, or selling a porperty that the installation meets the BS7671 Wiring Regulations, and so a certificate will be issued upon a satisfactory result.
